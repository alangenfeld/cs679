Future advice to students.

  • Make sure your development process is interative so you'll always have a game no present.
  • Make sure you have enough knowledge and skill to achive the first iteration.
  • Remember you have other classes and maybe work so don't aim too high.
  • Try something new and different. Although you can't make a AAA title you can still put a prototype out of an inovative game idea.
  • Know your own and your team's strengths and weaknesses then design a game with that in mind.
  • Split your game project into decoupled parts and have each person work on a part.
