The beginning to the adventure.

The afterlife, in a way it's the largest myster we must face. Some think we go to a paradise, others believe there is no afterlife. After being dead and mining oar in the brutal shadow world for 10 years.
You found a small passage way.

Without pause or hesitation, knowing anything would be better that continuing to work, you throw yourself into the passage only to look for another way out.

How to play.

Use the W A S and D keys to move up, left, down, and right respectively.
Dodge the shadows and explore the caverns for an exit.

The Golden Release

After 4 weeks of countless all nighters, debating over the smallest design decision, and writing wall upon walls of text on the white boards. Now we are finally ready to release.
Please enjoy our golden release of The Shadow Maze

Cool stuff not to miss.

  • Awesome home made shadows.
