Welcome to the playtest of spooky untitled game.

You are lost and helpless in a dark world. You are trying to explore to find why you are in this dark world.


Movement: use WASD or the arrow keys to move


The goal of the game in its current state is to find the magic rotating place holder cube. On your way your sanity will be challenged by spooky creatures.
